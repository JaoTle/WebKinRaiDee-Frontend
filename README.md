## Random food menu 
Random food menu then show how to cook or delivery channel for someone who is bored and doesn't know what to eat.

## Responsible
- First page 
  - Recommend food
  - Click food to see food info
- Random Page
  - Random result
  - Food rank that people likes
  - Button link to grab
  - Like Button
- Sign Up Page
- Random History Page
